**Answer:** C: Pit and fissure sealants  

Clinical reasoning: Pit and fissure sealants have been extensively studied and shown to be highly effective in preventing dental caries by sealing off deep pits and grooves where bacteria accumulate. They are both cost-effective and clinically validated as one of the most successful preventive interventions for pediatric dental caries.
