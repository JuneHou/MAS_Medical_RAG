**Answer:** D: Stress relieving heat treatment  

Annealing is a thermal processing technique used to reduce internal stresses in metals or materials by heating them to high temperatures and then cooling slowly. This process relieves residual stress, which can otherwise lead to warping, cracking, or reduced mechanical properties—making it equivalent to "stress-relieving" rather than strengthening (hard hardening). Hence, the correct term among the choices is *Stress relieving heat treatment*.
