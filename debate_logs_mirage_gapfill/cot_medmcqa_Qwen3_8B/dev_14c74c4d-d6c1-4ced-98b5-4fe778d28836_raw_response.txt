**Answer:** B: Phenylbutyrate  

Phenylbutyrate is used in urea cycle disorders to provide an alternative pathway for ammonia detoxification by acting as an amino group acceptor, forming glutamine that can be excreted. This helps lower blood ammonia levels in patients with these metabolic disorders.
