**Answer:** C: Middle meningeal artery  

The pharyngotympanic (Eustachian) tubes receive blood supply from branches of the ascending pharyngeal, ascending palatine, and arterial pterygoid canals. The middle meningeal artery primarily supplies dural structures of the skull base and does not contribute to the vascularization of the Eustachian tube. Thus, it is excluded.
