**C**: To counteract the movement of denture which is caused during engagement of retentive arm.  
Reciprocal clasps help stabilize the denture by opposing the forces generated when the retentive arm engages, thus preventing dislodgement due to functional movements like chewing or speaking. This design enhances retention and stability in removable partial dentures.
