I agree with the initial conclusion that **morphine should not be used for ischemic pain**, as it may increase the risk of worsening myocardial ischemia by causing systemic vasodilation. One additional reason is that morphine also reduces heart rate via vagal stimulation, potentially decreasing cardiac output and increasing oxygen demand relative to supply in an already compromised system.
