**Answer:** B: Lateral displacement of the condyle (or) Lateral fracture dislocation condyle  
**Clinical Reasoning:** Open reduction is absolutely required when there is lateral displacement or dislocation of the condyle because it leads to significant joint instability, malalignment, and risk of post-traumatic arthritis. Without correction, functional impairment and long-term disability are likely; thus, this represents an absolute surgical indication.
