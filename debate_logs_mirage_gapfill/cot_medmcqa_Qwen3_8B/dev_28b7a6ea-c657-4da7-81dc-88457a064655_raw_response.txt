**Answer:** A: Milnacipran  

Milnacipran is a serotonin-norepinephrine reuptake inhibitor (SNRI) primarily used for treating fibromyalgia and chronic pain, with no established role in managing osteoporosis. In contrast, parathyroid hormone (PTH), strontium ranelate, and denosumab are all approved therapies for increasing bone density or reducing fracture risk in patients with osteoporosis.
