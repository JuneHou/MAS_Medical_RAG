Answer: **C: Hypothalamus**  

The hypothalamus serves as the primary integrative center for autonomic nervous system regulation, coordinating responses such as temperature control, hunger, and stress via connections with the brainstem and spinal cord. It acts as a bridge between the central nervous system and peripheral autonomic outflow through the sympathetic and parasympathetic divisions.
