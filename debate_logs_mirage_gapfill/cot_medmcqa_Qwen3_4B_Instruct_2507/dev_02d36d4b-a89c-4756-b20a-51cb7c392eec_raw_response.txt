**Answer:** B: aPTT  

The aPTT (activated partial thromboplastin time) specifically measures the function of factors involved in the intrinsic pathway of coagulation, including VIII, IX, XI, XII, and X. It is therefore the most appropriate test to assess the integrity of this pathway.
